My Daily Meal

Description

The meal contains microwavable rice, veges and pre-cooked chicken.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Watties mixed veges packet
  • Uncle Ben's microwavable rice

Steps

  1. Microwave the vege packet for 2 mins
  2. While veges in microwave, cook pre cooked chicken on hot pan with oil
  3. Take veges out when done, put rice in for 2 minutes
  4. When rice done, take out and put all together for meal yum and aweosme
